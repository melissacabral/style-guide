Tints & Shades

These colors are lighter and darker variations of our main brand colors. These are especially helpful for hover states, illustrations, and other small variations.

Neutral Base Tints and Shades

These tints are based off of a neutral color that supports our Brand colors. Use them for background-colors, where a little more depth is needed. They are subtle, so don't rely on them to visually distinguish separate elements. You can see them used as the alternating backgrounds on this style guide!
